Welcome to SRM NEXUS Version 1.0!

Discover a Thriving Campus Community
At SRM NEXUS, we are thrilled to present Version 1.0 of our dynamic platform designed to foster a vibrant campus community within SRM University. With a comprehensive collection of clubs and their detailed descriptions, we provide a seamless gateway to explore the diverse interests and passions that thrive on our campus.

Extensive Club Directory
Explore a wide range of clubs, spanning sports, arts, academics, culture, and community service, all conveniently listed on our platform. With just a few clicks, you can delve into the unique offerings and distinctive characteristics of each club, empowering you to find the perfect fit for your interests and aspirations.

Stay Ahead with Upcoming Events
Stay in the loop with our News section, where you can stay updated on all the exciting events and activities that campus clubs have in store for the coming days. Be the first to know about workshops, competitions, seminars, and more, as clubs share their plans and initiatives to keep you engaged and informed.

Catch the Highlights
On our Headline page, get a quick snapshot of all the clubs' upcoming events through engaging snippets. Be prepared to dive into an ocean of opportunities as you plan your journey of exploration and involvement within our university's thriving club ecosystem.

Opportunities Galore
We believe in connecting you with meaningful experiences. For every club, our cards feature a convenient hiring option, exclusively visible when they are actively recruiting new members. Elevate your university journey by seizing leadership roles, building skills, and creating lasting memories through club participation.

Customized News Section
Clubs' representatives have the power to personalize the News section, ensuring that you receive updates directly from the source. Be a part of the action and get real-time information straight from the clubs you love!

Bulletin Board (C-TECH Department)
Exclusive to C-TECH department students for all four years, the Bulletin Board provides vital updates about your department. Stay informed about the latest news and announcements shared with us on WhatsApp by our Faculty Advisor (FA).

Superset (3rd and 4th Year Students)
Tailored for 3rd and 4th-year students, the Superset offers insights into placement activities for third and fourth-year students respectively. Stay updated on job opportunities, campus interviews, and career guidance to kickstart your professional journey.
