Data Science Community SRM

DESCRIPTION

We are a technology-driven Data Science-based student-led innovation community at SRM IST. Our aim is to foster development and entrepreneurial skills among students and work as a community that inspires thousands.We make real-time projects, conduct research, organize meetups, host events, and workshops primarily focusing on Data Science, Machine Learning, Al, Deep Learning, MLOps, Web & App Development and more. We guide and teach all those who aspire to work in these fields and help them attain better skills and knowledge.
